About this course

AALOKAN-IAS has always believed in providing a qualitative yet quantitative solution to UPSC aspirants in order to enhance their preparation for the UPSC Civil Services Examination. Backed by years of experience and on-going research & analysis of the UPSC Civil Services Exam, we have developed the AALOKAN-IAS GS Foundation Course 2023-2024 which is in tune with dynamic nature of this examination as well as expectations of UPSC.

It is one of the most comprehensive programs offered by AALOKAN-IAS with an objective to provide the aspirants with a solid platform for their preparation and guide them in their path to success.

Why choose our program?
